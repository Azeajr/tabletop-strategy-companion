## Axis C — Model fit

Guess Who's decision structure is a single loop (ask Q → eliminate → repeat until guess), not a temporal arc. The linear phase model (Setup → Early → Mid → End) is a teaching progression that maps reasonably to the game's natural flow: setup (initial position), broad elimination (early), specific elimination (mid), final guess (end).

| # | model element | Finding | Disposition |

| C2 | Categories | Question Strategy, Defense, Guess Timing are logical buckets (your offense, your defense, guess timing). Alphabetical order (Defense → Guess Timing → Question) does not mislead. | Accept |
